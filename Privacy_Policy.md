# Privacy Policy for WatchMyMoney

**Last updated: November 21, 2025**

## Introduction
WatchMyMoney ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Wear OS application handles your information.

## Data Collection and Use
**We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on our servers.**

WatchMyMoney functions entirely as a standalone application on your Wear OS device.

### Local Data Storage
The application stores the following user-provided preferences locally on your device using Android DataStore:
*   **Annual Salary**: Used solely to calculate and display your real-time earnings.
*   **Currency Symbol**: Used for display purposes.
*   **Reset Hour**: Used to determine when the daily counter resets.

This data remains on your device and is removed if you uninstall the application or clear the application data.

## Third-Party Services
*   **No Analytics**: We do not use third-party analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase) to track your usage.
*   **No Advertising**: This application does not display advertisements and does not share data with advertising networks.

## Permissions
The application requests the following permissions for core functionality:
*   `WAKE_LOCK`: To keep the screen active during the ticker animation.
*   `BIND_COMPLICATION_PROVIDER`: To display your earnings on the watch face.

## Children's Privacy
Our application does not collect any personal information from children. It is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
